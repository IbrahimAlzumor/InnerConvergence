About Us

Oblivion Forge is a passionate game development studio dedicated to crafting immersive, story-rich experiences. Our first project, Inner Convergence, blends psychological intrigue, strategic gameplay, and a deep narrative that stays with you long after you play.

Inner Convergence

Inner Convergence is not just a game—it’s a journey. Follow the mystery of a cursed game discovered in an old lockbox, where the boundaries between reality and digital worlds blur.

Key Features:

  • Interactive narrative where your choices matter.
  • Unique chess gameplay with a haunting twist.
  • AI-driven characters with mysterious backstories.
