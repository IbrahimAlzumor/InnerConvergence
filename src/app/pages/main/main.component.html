At Oblivion Forge, we are a passionate and driven game development studio focused on creating immersive experiences that combine intricate stories, compelling gameplay, and unforgettable characters. Inner Convergence

Inner Convergence is more than a mere game; it’s a transformative journey where players are invited to unravel the layers of a dark and mysterious narrative. Dive into a world where a cursed game unearthed from obscurity takes center stage, blurring the lines between what is digital and what is real in an unsettling, yet enthralling way.

Key Features:

  • Immerse yourself in an interactive narrative experience where every choice you make significantly shapes the outcomes and determines the direction of the story.
  • Enjoy uniquely designed chess gameplay mechanics infused with eerie, haunting elements that heighten tension and engagement.
  • Interact with deeply detailed, AI-driven characters who each have rich and mysterious backstories that enhance immersion.
